Why This Exists

Research designed
for the ecosystem

Most data on India's startup ecosystem is a byproduct of something else — deal flow, signups, press. The questions get shaped by what the publisher wants the answers to say.

Theory of Next is built around a different standard. Research design is independent of any commercial interest in the findings. Questions are locked before fieldwork begins. Results are shared with participants, not selectively published.
